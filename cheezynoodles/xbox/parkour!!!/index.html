Parkour!!! - Xbox

Griffcraft & AJ

Download Map
Size: 1.1 MB

Parkour!!! is a parkour map. We know that sometimes when you spawn in Xbox maps, you often spawn outside of the lobby. If you follow the glowstone outside the map, it will lead you to a door to the lobby. If you play this map in a video, we would really appreciate it if you put a link to this page in the description. This map was built and tested on a Xbox360, but should also work on a Xbox One. We hope you enjoy!
-Griffcraft & AJ
